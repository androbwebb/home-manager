# Hearth

**The AI maintenance coordinator for short-term rentals, small landlords, and homeowners.**

Hearth is an AI agent that knows every part of your property — the HVAC model, the filter size, the paint color, the warranty docs, the plumber you trust — and actually does the work to keep it running. Tell Hearth "the split unit needs new filters" and it orders the right ones, schedules the contractor, confirms the work, and updates your records. No spreadsheets, no group texts with handymen, no buying the wrong filter again.

---

## The Problem

Property maintenance is death by a thousand small tasks. For STR hosts juggling turnovers, small landlords with a handful of doors, or homeowners managing a primary residence, the same friction repeats:

- You don't remember the model number of the dishwasher in unit 3
- You bought the wrong filter size — again
- The "good plumber" is buried in a text thread from 2023
- Preventive maintenance slips until something breaks
- Every contractor request is a new round of phone tag, quotes, and chasing

Existing tools either remind you to do things (HomeZada, Centriq) or track work orders after you've already done the coordinating (Buildium, Breezeway). Nothing actually executes.

Hearth does.

## How It Works

### 1. Build your property's brain
Walk through each property once. Photograph appliances, equipment, and finishes. Hearth's vision model identifies make, model, and serial number, then automatically pulls manuals, warranty info, and replacement part catalogs. Add your preferred contractors, paint codes, and access instructions. Done in roughly an hour per unit.

### 2. Talk to it like a person
> "The split unit in the master bedroom of the Waikiki condo needs new filters."

Hearth knows which unit, which filter size, where you usually buy, and what your spending cap is. It drafts an order, shows you the price, and waits for one tap to confirm.

> "Guest reported the dishwasher isn't draining at 412 Maple."

Hearth checks the model, runs through the common fixes, and if it can't be solved remotely, dispatches your preferred plumber (or finds a licensed one via Thumbtack) with the model details, access code, and guest schedule already in the message.

### 3. It runs the boring stuff on autopilot
Filters every 90 days. HVAC seasonal service. Smoke detector batteries. Gutter cleaning before the rainy season. Hearth schedules, orders, and confirms — you just approve.
